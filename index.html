Park Bom

Park Bom (born March 24, 1984) is a South Korean singer.[1] She was born in Seoul, South Korea, and later moved to the United States as a child where she learned English. She is a member of the South Korean girl group 2NE1, which is under YG Entertainment.[2] Starting in 2006, Park recorded with labelmates Big Bang, Lexy, Masta Wu and starred with Lee Hyori in her CF, "Anystar" as a co-actress. Later in 2008, she starred as the lead actress in Kim Ji-eun's music video, "Tell Me Once More". In 2009, she debuted with 2NE1 as the main vocalist. Park has released two solo singles, namely "You and I" and "Don't Cry". Both singles reached number one on the Gaon Digital Chart, the national music chart of South Korea. "You and I" also won "Best Digital Single" at the Mnet Asian Music Awards in 2010.
